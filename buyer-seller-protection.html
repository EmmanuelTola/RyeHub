Protecting Our Users

We know sometimes things don’t go smoothly as planned. Therefore, if an eligible item you’ve bought on RyeHub doesn’t arrive or doesn’t match the seller’s description, we can reimburse you for the full purchase amount of the item plus postage.

Buyer's Protection

If an eligible item that you’ve bought doesn’t arrive, or doesn’t match the seller’s description, RyeHub's Buyer Protection may reimburse you for the full amount of the item plus postage. Buyer Protection can cover your eligible purchases on RyeHub. Conditions apply.

What's Covered

Tangible / Physical Items or goods that can be posted, received through mail, delivery or digitally. Cases could include; receiving old or used item if you purchased a new one, getting less than quantity of product purchased, damaged product/missing parts, product doesn’t fit seller’s description.

What's Not Covered

Intangible items like services, real estate, motorized vehicles (of any kind), custom made items, travel tickets, industrial machinery (for manufacturing), prepaid or gift cards, anything you buy in person, or items that violate RyeHub’s policies

Guideline

  • If purchase is confirmed (by buyer), dispute must be opened within 7 days
  • If purchase is automatically confirmed, dispute must be opened within 30 days
  • Full item cost has to be covered in one payment
  • Buyers cannot open several disputes for the same payment

    • Seller's Protection

    If a buyer claims he never received his item or pays for an unauthorized/invalidated quote, our Seller Protection can help you get/keep the full amount of an eligible payment, subject of the Claim, Chargeback, or Reversal and waive the Chargeback Fee, if applicable. As long as you meet the eligibility requirements, you may be protected.

    What's Covered

    Seller Protection covers tangible and intangible items (including services travel and tickets) purchased on RyeHub and, where applicable, shipped to the shipping address registered during the transaction (see our User Agreement).

    Documents Required

    A - Tangible items: Keep the Proof of Postage (minimum) or Proof of Delivery for the goods that you sell. They may be requested in case of a dispute.

    B - Intangible items (including services travel and tickets): We will ask you to provide Proof of Delivery to show the purchase order was fulfilled and includes all of the following:

  • The date the item or service was provided
  • The recipient’s address (email/IP, etc.) where applicable

    • What's Not Covered

  • Items that are not shipped to the recipient address. This includes items redirected after inital wrong shipping
  • Financial products and investments
  • Counterfeit goods.
  • Items equivalent to cash (including, without limitation, gift cards or vouchers).

    • We value your safety

    No matter where you shop, our security is always the same. So buy with peace of mind from hundreds of stores on RyeHub.

    Chargebacks

    A buyer may request a chargeback or fund reversal from RyeHub or PayPal, but cannot pursue both at the same time or seek a double recovery. RyeHub may put a hold on the funds in the seller’s account to cover a request. We're here to help and will inform you of every step of the process to resolve a case.

    24/7 Security

    We monitor every transaction 24/7 to help prevent against fraud, email phishing and identity theft. And remember, we never ask for any sensitive information from you in an email.

    Secure Information

    When you make a purchase on RyeHub, your financial information is never shared with the seller, and you can pay using only your email address and a confirmation code. If you lose access to your email address, you can contact us
